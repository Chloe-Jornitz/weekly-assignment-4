Carreer

Carreer:

Software engineering

Description:

Software engineering uses both engineering principles and programming to build or modify computer software.
Software engineers:
  • - Analyze the requirements for a project and decide what the software must do
  • - Design and plan software solutions using diagrams, flow charts, or models
  • - Use different tools and programming languages to write code
  • - Test code to assure that it functions correctly
  • - Find issues in code and fix code so that it runs smoothly and efficiently
  • - Create reports to keep track of changes and developments to projects
  • - Work collaboratively with other software engineers
They may create:
  • - Computer programs
  • - Computer software
  • - Websites
  • - Computer games
  • - Applications
  • - Operating systems

Education requirements:

Most workplaces search for software engineers with at least a bachelor’s degree in one of the following disciplines:
  • - Software engineering
  • - Computer science
  • - Information technology
  • - Computer systems engineering
  • - Mathematics
  • - Other subjects related to software engineering

Future possibilities:

Demand is increasing for software engineers as the need for application, website, and software development as well as the use of artificial intelligence is rising. Because of this, most of the job openings in this career will come from new positions created by the expansion of the software industry. Growing need for mobile applications, medical software, AI applications and cybersecurity are examples of factors that increase the demand for software engineers.

25% growth by 2032

Why am I interested in this career?:

I believe that a career related to computer science would suit me because I enjoy programming and would like to continue to learn more about programming and improve my knowledge with different programming languages. I like solving problems and the challenges that programming provides. I also like that in programming that there are multiple different ways to complete a task. A career in software engineering specifically interests me because I have considered having a career in engineering, as my favourite subjects in school are math and science and I typically do well in these areas. University Program

Educational institution:

York University - Keele Campus
This program is offered from from Lassonde School of Engineering.

Program:

Software engineering

Length of program:

5 years (including co-op)

Entrance requirements:

  • - Ontario Secondary School Diploma (OSSD)
  • - Grade 12 english university level course (ENG4U)
  • - Grade 12 chemistry university level course (SCH4U)
  • - Grade 12 physics university level course (SPH4U)
  • - Grade 12 advanced functions university level course (MHF4U)
  • - Grade 12 calculus and vectors course (MCV4U)
  • - One more grade 12 university or mixed course (4U or M)
  • - All prerequisite courses must have a grade of 70% or higher
  • - Should have an average in the low to mid 80s to be admitted into this program

Some courses in this program:

  • - Advanced Object-Oriented Programming
  • - Software Development Project
  • - Design and Analysis of Algorithms
  • - Operating System Fundamentals
  • - Software Design
  • - Software Engineering Testing
  • - Mission Critical Systems
  • - Building E-Commerce Systems

Other requirements to complete this degree:

  • - Students will need to work together in teams to complete a capstone project that will demonstrate all of their knowledge learned in this course
  • - Co-op and internships are available after second year to gain experience to become a licensed engineer. Some co-op employers include:
    • - IBM
    • - Ericsson
    • - BMO
    • - Nascent
    • - Ontario Ministry of Health and Long-Term Care
    • - Loyalty One
    • - Teledyne
    • - Telus
  • - This program must be completed full-time

The Vocation of Humanity

How does this career help fulfill the vocation of humanity?

Software engineers create software and applications that can be used for many purposes. For example, the use of software in the medical field is increasing and software engineers can help create medical software. Medical software helps increase the accuracy of medical diagnoses, improve treatment of diseases or injuries and can save lives. Computer software also makes websites possible for many helpful companies and organizations. For example, people are now able to donate to charities online. This can increase the amount of money donated to people in need. People will be more likely to donate because it is more convenient, as they do not have to go out of their way to donate the money in person, but they can donate easily and quickly from home. Websites and online advertising can also spread awareness for world issues and make people realize the work that needs to be done to make the world a better place. God calls us to live our lives to our full potential and to help the less fortunate. Software engineers make this possible by advancing medical technology to allow people more time to accomplish God’s purpose for them and giving people opportunities to share their wealth and knowledge with others. This calling to reach my full potential and help others does help me to set priorities in school, work and my personal life. I know that I need to set my school work as a high priority so that I can get the grades necessary to get into the university program that I want, to both get a job that will bring me success and happiness and to gain new knowledge and understanding about the world. I know that I need to work hard and responsibly in my career so that I can do my job properly and be a good, trustworthy co-worker and employee. Finally, I know that I need to put the needs of others before myself when someone is struggling or needs support.
